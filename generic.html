General Info about Laptops

5.17.2017

A laptop is built up of many components, those include but are not limited to

Lithium based batteries. They have an average lifespan of 2-3 years when used regularly.

A microprocessor, made of silicon. It sends commands to the processor

A HDD or SSD, holds all the storage including but not limited to: your Operating System, pictures and preference, SSDs are more likely to be used for running OS because they transfer data faster than HDDs

A HDD is a physical disk and reader while a SSD is purely digital making it faster than a HDD

RAM Short for Random Acess Memories is your computers short term memory and allows your computer to acess the programs it was recently running
